Projects

Play as Sam and fish to your hearts content. Sell fish you've caught, collect better gear, and use it to explore the depths of the mysterious lake, but be warned as no one knows what lies at the bottom. Wretched Depths is a PSX-style horror fishing game created by students at the University of Utah.

Wretched Depths

Create a world of your own while playing as Talise, a peaceful witch who is on a mission to get to the big city. Enjoy learning the concepts of chemistry by farming for rare ingredients and creating new concoctions using your cauldron an in effort to get the local train up and running again. Khestoria is a pixel-art farming simulator with the intent of teaching chemistry.

Khestoria

Explore a long forgotten island to discover the screts it holds, but beware, you are not the only one who is lurking. Raising Hell is a first person survival horror game set on a remote island in the 1600s.

Raising Hell

Venture under down into the sea and enjoy a peaceful ambiance while hunting for your next meal! Project: Exploding Barnacles is a VR experience that allows the player to explore the depths through a deep-sea spearfishing experience.

Project: Exploding Barnacle

Ceareal Killer is a lighthearted Machinima project that explores the world of a hard working "Monster" who can't seem to catch a break in the competitive market of being a cereal mascot.
