Project: Exploding Barnacle

Venture under down into the sea and enjoy a peaceful ambiance while hunting for your next meal! Project: Exploding Barnacles is a VR experience that allows the player to explore the depths through a deep-sea spearfishing experience.

This was my first dive into creating a VR experience and it helped me to learn a ton about interactive development! This project was created by a team of four using Unity's built in XR support and testing through the Unity Mock HMD as well as a real head mounted display that a group member had.

For my contributions, I specifically was tasked with designing and modeling the speargun. The model was made and UV Mapped within Maya, with the model being based off the real life Riffe Marauder speargun. After this, I used Substance Painter to texture the speargun.

I worked on the base for a realistic speargun shooting experience, directional movement to make movement smoother so the user would feel as though they were swimming, and designing a relaxing and realistic scenery which was done using an Unreal asset pack that was ported over and textured within Unity.

Overall, this project was a ton of fun to work on and gave me some great experience in VR development. Screenshots of the overall experience can be seen below.
