Cereal Killer

Cereal Killer is a lighthearted Machinima project that explores the world of a hard working "Monster" who can't seem to catch a break in the competitive market of being a cereal mascot.

For this project, I worked in a group of eight people to create a silly, lighthearted animated project. My role on the team was to develop the introductory scene up to the title screen of the animation. This included working in Unreal Engine 4 to create the basic animation timeline. I created and textured the cereal box within Maya and utilized models and animations from Mixamo.

Overall, this was a fun project to introduce myself into animations using Unreal and our team had a great time during the project! The video can be seen down below, thanks for watching!
