Hello! My name is Ian Gerrits, I just graduated from the University of Utah with a Bachelors in Computer Science with an emphasis in Entertainment Arts and Engineering. I have always had a passion for computers, playing on them since I was about two years old and building them since I was twelve. I currently have a solid background in the field of IT but am looking to shift over to the world of Software Engineering or Game Development, as I am very interested in both.

Outside of work and computers, I love to cook, spend time with my family and listen to music. It goes without saying, I also love video games with some of my all time favorite titles being Final Fantasy X for having one of the most fantastic stories in all of gaming and Resident Evil 7 for creating one of the best horror experiences I have seen. I hope you enjoy my portfolio!
